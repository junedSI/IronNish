About Nishant Bharadwaj


Welcome to the next chapter of your fitness journey! I'm Nishant Bharadwaj, and I'm here to help you transform your dreams into reality. As a fitness influencer, dedicated trainer, knowledgeable nutritionist, and with a strong understanding of medicine, I bring a wealth of experience and passion to the table.

From 18, I was captivated by the incredible potential of the human body. This fascination led me to dive deep into the world of fitness, exploring the realms of workouts, nutrition, and holistic wellness.
